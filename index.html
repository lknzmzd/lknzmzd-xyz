About LKNZMZD Engineering Lab

LKNZMZD is an independent robotics and embedded systems engineering lab founded by Ilkin Azimzade. The lab focuses on electro-mechanical prototyping, motion systems, smart automation devices, and experimental robotic platforms.

Core Focus Areas

  • Robotics prototypes (humanoid systems, kinetic mechanisms)
  • Embedded systems engineering and firmware development
  • Servo control, motion design, and electro-mechanical integration
  • AI + hardware experiments (edge computing, perception prototypes)
  • Rapid prototyping with 3D printing and custom parts

Tools & Technologies

Arduino, ESP32, Raspberry Pi, NVIDIA Jetson, sensors and actuators, 3D printing workflows, and web-based interactive demos (Three.js).
